# Tone Of Voice

Customer and operator messaging should be calm, precise, and action-oriented. Responses should explain what was found, what is still uncertain, and what the next safe step is without sounding evasive or alarmist.

Summaries should avoid overstating certainty. If the system had to truncate data, redact fields, or request approval, that fact should be conveyed clearly and without blame.

Drafted messages should remain professional and readable for non-specialists. Favor concrete language, short sentences, and explicit follow-up steps.
